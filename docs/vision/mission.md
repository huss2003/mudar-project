# Mission

Transform approximately 10,000 companies every week into only 20–30 evidence-backed, broker-ready commercial real estate opportunities.

## What This Means

**10,000 companies per week is the input.** This is the approximate number of registered companies in Pune's commercial real estate relevant sectors — IT/ITES, manufacturing, BFSI, professional services, healthcare, and e-commerce. The platform does not select which companies to analyze; it systematically processes the entire addressable market. Every company gets evaluated, scored, and either qualified or disqualified based on evidence.

**20–30 opportunities is the output.** This is the number of companies per week that have strong, verifiable signals of needing commercial space within 90 days. These are not guesses or probabilities in the abstract — each opportunity is backed by specific evidence: hiring surges, lease expirations, expansion announcements, organizational restructuring, or growth funding. The broker can call each lead with specific context and a credible value proposition.

**Evidence-backed is the constraint.** A company does not qualify as an opportunity simply because it is growing. It qualifies because the Evidence Engine has verified specific, actionable signals. Hiring 50 engineers in Hinjewadi is a signal. Having a lease expiring in 4 months is a signal. Being acquired and needing to integrate a new team is a signal. Each signal is sourced, verified, and weighted.

**Broker-ready means the broker can take action immediately.** Every delivered lead includes decision-maker contacts, company context, the specific signals detected, and a recommended outreach strategy. The broker does not need to research the company before calling. The research is already done, verified, and packaged.

## Why This Mission Matters

This mission inverts the traditional brokerage model. Instead of the broker actively searching for opportunities, the platform delivers curated, verified opportunities to the broker. The broker's job shifts from prospecting to advising — from finding leads to closing them.

The mission also defines what the platform will not do. It will not maximize lead quantity. It will not deliver unverified lists. It will not optimize for volume metrics. Every company that does not meet the evidence threshold is dropped, and that is by design. The mission prioritizes precision over recall, confidence over coverage, and broker productivity over database size.
