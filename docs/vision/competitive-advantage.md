# Competitive Advantage

The Jasfo platform competes not against other software products but against the institutional research teams of large brokerages. Its competitive advantages stem from a fundamentally different approach to lead generation — one that is systematic, evidence-driven, and cost-optimized rather than relationship-dependent and labor-intensive.

## Evidence-Driven vs. Relationship-Driven

**Traditional brokers rely on relationships.** A broker's pipeline depends on who they know, who remembers them, and who thinks of them when space needs arise. This model works for the top 200 relationships but leaves the remaining 4,800+ companies in the market invisible. It is also fragile — a broker changing firms or a key contact leaving their company can eliminate years of relationship building.

**Jasfo relies on evidence.** The platform monitors 10,000 companies systematically, detecting growth signals, hiring patterns, organizational changes, and lease events through public data. A company's likelihood to need space is determined by what it is doing, not by who knows the broker. This evidence-driven approach is scalable, consistent, and survives personnel changes on either side of the transaction.

**The advantage is structural.** An institutional research team might monitor 500–1,000 companies in depth. Jasfo monitors the entire addressable market. The platform does not get tired, does not have off days, and does not overlook companies outside the broker's network.

## AI-Native vs. Manual

**Institutional research teams are expensive.** A five-person research team in Pune costs INR 25–40 lakhs per year in salaries alone. This cost is viable for a large brokerage with 50+ brokers but prohibitive for a solo operator. The research team's output is also inconsistent — dependent on individual analyst skill, motivation, and methodology.

**Jasfo is AI-native.** The entire pipeline is automated. AI agents do the research, verification, scoring, and packaging. The platform costs under $50/month. A solo broker gets the output of a research team at 1–2% of the cost.

**The advantage is growing.** AI inference costs are declining approximately 50% per year. The capabilities of open-weight models like DeepSeek V4 Flash are improving rapidly. The platform's cost advantage over human research teams will widen, not narrow, over time.

## Cost-Optimized vs. Enterprise Tools

**Enterprise CRE tech is expensive.** Platforms like CoStar, Reonomy, and CompStak cost $500–5,000/month. They are designed for US markets and large brokerage teams with dedicated budgets. They focus on property-level data (comps, listings, ownership) rather than company-level intelligence (growth signals, space needs, decision-makers).

**Jasfo is cost-optimized from the ground up.** Every architectural decision is measured against the $50/month budget. The layered pipeline design with cost gates ensures that money is only spent where it creates value. The platform does not charge per-seat, per-lead, or per-API-call because it has no product pricing — it is a proprietary tool.

**The advantage is structural, not temporary.** Enterprise tools are expensive because they are sold as products with sales teams, marketing, and customer support. Jasfo has none of these costs. Its cost advantage is built into the business model, not optimized away.

## Leading Indicator Focus vs. Lagging-Only

**Most brokers react to lagging indicators.** A company lists a space requirement. A building has a "For Lease" sign. A competitor announces they are representing a new tenant. These are all signals that the opportunity has already passed — other brokers are already competing.

**Jasfo focuses on leading indicators.** Hiring surges, lease expirations, organizational restructuring, funding announcements, and expansion plans. These signals precede the public space requirement by weeks or months. The broker arrives at the conversation before the competition knows the conversation exists.

**The advantage compounds.** Being first means the broker can advise rather than pitch. Instead of competing on commission rate or showing the same properties as three other brokers, Jasfo's broker arrives with market intelligence, space recommendations, and a consultative approach. Companies value this expertise and reward it with exclusive mandates.
