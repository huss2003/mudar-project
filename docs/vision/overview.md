# Vision Overview

Jasfo is an AI-native commercial real estate intelligence platform purpose-built for a solo broker operating in the Pune market. It transforms the way commercial real estate leads are discovered, qualified, and acted upon by replacing manual research with a systematic, evidence-driven intelligence pipeline. The platform ingests approximately 10,000 companies per week and distills them into 20–30 broker-ready opportunities, each backed by verified evidence and a confidence score.

## The Core Concept

Commercial real estate brokerage has traditionally been a relationship-driven business. Brokers rely on personal networks, market reputation, and manual research to identify companies that may need space. This approach works well for the top-tier institutional firms that have dedicated research teams, but it puts solo brokers and small firms at a structural disadvantage. Jasfo reverses this dynamic by building an AI-augmented intelligence layer that does the work of a full research team at a fraction of the cost.

The platform operates on a simple premise: collect everything, verify everything, reason carefully, spend money only when justified, deliver evidence not guesses. Every company that enters the pipeline is scraped, normalized, verified, analyzed by multiple specialist AI agents, and evaluated by a final reasoning judge before being presented as a lead. No gut feelings, no hunches, no unsupported assumptions.

## The Pune Market Context

The Pune commercial real estate market is uniquely suited to this approach. With over 5,000 active companies across IT/ITES, manufacturing, BFSI, and professional services, the market is large enough to require systematic intelligence but fragmented enough that no single broker can maintain complete awareness through relationships alone. Most commercial real estate transactions in Pune are still driven by outbound broker outreach, meaning that being first to identify a company's space need is often the deciding factor in winning the mandate.

Jasfo targets this information asymmetry. By monitoring thousands of companies continuously — tracking hiring patterns, expansion announcements, lease expirations, organizational changes, and growth signals — the platform identifies space needs weeks or months before they become public listings. The broker arrives at the conversation informed, prepared, and ahead of every competitor.

## Guiding Philosophy

The platform is built on five core principles. Evidence First means every AI conclusion must cite its source. Firecrawl First means the primary scraper is always the first choice. AI First means every decision is made by AI before human review. Free APIs First means paid services are reserved only for qualified leads. Quality over Quantity means twenty verified opportunities are more valuable than ten thousand unqualified company names.

These principles inform every architectural decision, from the 14-layer pipeline design to the model routing strategy to the cost-gating mechanism. The result is a platform that maximizes lead quality and broker productivity while operating within a strict monthly budget.
